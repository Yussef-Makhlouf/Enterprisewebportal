✓ Do
  • Use full colour logo on white, Pearl, or Indigo
  • Use reverse logo (white) on dark backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space = ½ width of GIG "G"
  • Use single-colour Indigo logo on light backgrounds
  • Write brand name in Title Case: Gulf Insurance Group
  • Write initialism in uppercase: GIG
✗ Don't
  • Don't outline, rotate, or add effects to logo
  • Don't stretch or squash the logo
  • Don't place full-colour logo on dark photos
  • Don't use any logo on busy backgrounds
  • Don't use reverse logo on Pearl backgrounds
  • Don't create new logo compositions
  • Don't use logo in secondary colours

08 · Visual Language
Photography Principles

GIG photography captures authentic human moments. Two modes: Masterbrand (emotive, elegant) and Subsidiary / Retail (everyday, approachable).

01
Capture Real Life

Use photography that feels natural rather than artificial. Capture signs of humanity — whether using people, places, or objects.

02
Avoid Clutter

Avoid clutter through intelligent compositions. Aim to use images with one clear focal point. Single subject creates impact.

03
Control Tone

Control tones to create understated elegance. Use a shallow depth of field. Avoid too many contrasting colours in a single image.
