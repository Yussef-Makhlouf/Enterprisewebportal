Redesign the existing GIG (Gulf Insurance Group) dashboard UI to fully align with the official GIG Brand & Identity Guidelines (Version 9, June 2022). Apply all the following brand specifications precisely:

---

### COLOUR SYSTEM

**Primary Colour:**
- Indigo: RGB(25, 5, 140) — Use as dominant background, primary nav, headers, key UI surfaces
- Tints: Indigo 80%, 60% for secondary surfaces and hover states

**Functional Colours:**
- Pearl: RGB(229, 229, 229) — Light backgrounds, cards, dividers
- Deep Blue: Use in gradients mixed with Indigo for depth backgrounds

**Secondary Palette:**
- Ocean: RGB(128, 148, 230) — Charts, data highlights, secondary buttons
- Sunrise: RGB(255, 115, 102) — Alerts, warnings, accent CTAs
- Seafoam: RGB(107, 202, 186) — Success states, positive data indicators

**Special Accent:**
- Rose-Gold — Use exclusively for the brand bar (bottom strip), premium badges, and key CTA buttons

**Gradient Usage:**
- Dark backgrounds: Deep Blue → Indigo (top to bottom)
- Light backgrounds: Pearl → White (subtle depth)
- Do NOT use Indigo directly on top of Rose-Gold

---

### TYPOGRAPHY

**Headlines & Display text:**
- Font: Reforma (Gris weight for large display, Blanca for standard headers)
- Use for: Page titles, section headers, KPI values, key metrics
- Style: Bold, left-aligned, Indigo or White depending on background

**Body & Functional Text:**
- Font: Georama (Regular for body, Medium for labels and sub-headings)
- Use for: Table content, descriptions, navigation labels, form fields, captions
- All uppercase for small functional labels and captions

**Arabic Text:**
- Headlines: Kufam (Bold/Semi-Bold)
- Body: Almarai (Regular/Bold)
- Support full RTL layout

**System Fallbacks:**
- Latin: Verdana
- Arabic: Tahoma

---

### GRAPHIC DEVICES & UI ELEMENTS

**Brand Bar:**
- A thin Rose-Gold horizontal bar (4–6px) at the very bottom of the dashboard shell or at the footer of cards

**Corner Device / Holding Shape:**
- Apply the GIG Corner Device (curved corner cut derived from the GIG wordmark geometry) to cards, modals, image containers, and key widget frames
- Use it as the primary card shape instead of simple rounded rectangles

**Background Graphic Shapes:**
- Use subtle crops of the GIG Brand Symbol (sea shell geometry) as background texture tiles on the main dashboard hero area or sidebar — low opacity (8–12%), in Indigo on dark or Pearl on light

**Secondary Pattern:**
- Use the GIG wordmark-derived geometric pattern as a background texture on data panels, footers, or section dividers — as a glaze surface at low opacity

---

### ICON SYSTEM

- All icons: Linear outline style, simple geometric construction
- On dark/Indigo backgrounds: White linework, no holding shape
- On light/Pearl backgrounds: Indigo linework, no holding shape
- For emphasis: Place icon in a circular Indigo or Ocean holding shape
- Icon categories to include: Property, Travel, Motor, Marine, Life, Medical, Location, Awards, Rating

---

### DATA VISUALISATION

**Charts & Graphs:**
- Bar charts: Use Indigo as primary bar, Ocean as secondary, Seafoam as tertiary, Sunrise for warnings
- Tints (50%) for when 5+ values need comparison
- Line graphs: Indigo primary line, Ocean secondary
- Pie/Donut charts: Indigo, Ocean, Seafoam, Sunrise, Rose-Gold slices
- All chart axes and labels in Georama Regular

---

### LAYOUT & COMPOSITION

**Navigation (Sidebar or Top Nav):**
- Background: Indigo
- Active state: Deep Blue or lighter Indigo tint with Rose-Gold left accent bar
- Text: White in Georama Medium
- Logo: GIG Full Corporate Signature (white/reverse version) in top-left

**Cards & Widgets:**
- Background: White or Pearl
- Border radius: Follow GIG Corner Device geometry (not standard rounded corners)
- Shadow: Subtle, no heavy drop shadows
- Header accent: Thin Indigo top border OR Rose-Gold brand bar at bottom

**Buttons:**
- Primary CTA: Indigo fill, white Georama Medium text
- Secondary: Ocean fill or outlined Indigo
- Hover state: Indigo 80% tint
- Special/Premium action: Rose-Gold fill

**Tables:**
- Header row: Indigo background, white Georama Medium text
- Alternating rows: White / Pearl
- Selected row: Ocean at 20% opacity
- Borders: Pearl dividers only

**Status Indicators:**
- Positive / Success: Seafoam
- Warning: Sunrise
- Error / Alert: Sunrise at full saturation
- Neutral: Pearl or Deep Blue 50%

---

### TONE & PHILOSOPHY

The dashboard should feel:
- Human and approachable, not clinical or cold
- Premium but not exclusive — "Invested in You" mindset
- Clean compositions with one clear focal point per section
- Understated elegance — avoid clutter, use generous white space
- Brand positioning tagline "Invested in You." should appear as a subtle element in the welcome/hero area
